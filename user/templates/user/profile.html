April

booking-item 1: the idea

Albert and Max one day met for a coffee talking about music. The idea emerged that they wanted to build place where musicians can meet up and share their interest and devotion for music. Practicing instruments, meet the peer group. Share excitement for own compositions. They decided to make the first move.

30

May

booking-item 2: the purchase

We found an old building that felt just right for what we had in mind. Sure, there was a lot of work ahead - but we were determined to bring it to life.

12

June

booking-item 3: the hard work

There was a ton of work to do. First, we tackled the electrics - good music gear needs stable power, especially those AMPs that love to gobble up energy. Next came the bathrooms and the cafeteria. Albert was happy - finally, there was coffee at the center.

25

July

booking-item 4: the rehearsal rooms are ready!

That was definitely the most expensive and time-consuming part of the whole project. Which room should host which instruments? Honestly - until then, we'd never really thought about how pricey music gear actually is. Not to mention the sound isolation, acoustic panels, paint, flooring... you name it. But in the end? We pulled it off - and pretty well, too.

12

August

booking-item 5: Opening Party

An unforgettable moment -almost 50 people showed up to our opening party. And not just for the barbecue and beer. SCHALLPLATTE is finally up and running!
